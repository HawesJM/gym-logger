Our Mission

To facilitate your exercising lifestyle by creating an interactive, shareable and browsable repository of your workouts and those created & logged by other users. With Gym Logger you can not only record each workout you do, but you can also edit your workouts, save those of other users for your own reference, and even set the workouts of others as a challenge for yourself the next time you hit the gym.

Form Entry Tips

Be as specific as possible regarding your 5 loggable exercises per workout session
The details can always be edited later if you've missed something.
You may use the "additional information" in the log form for information such as number of reps in a set, how many sets done etc.

FAQs

Does Gym Logger require a login? Yes you must create a free account to use our platform.
Can I keep my workout details private? Yes you can make each recorded workout private and visible only to you.
